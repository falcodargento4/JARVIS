Manual:
Use the fingers to control your computer:
Use one finger to control windows
Use two fingers to control volume
Use three fingers to use the scroll
Use four fingers to get a quick help

The dominant finger is highlighted by a circle
_____________________________________
One finger, Window:
Swipe right to select the right window,
slide down your finger in the red box to reduce the window
slide up your finger in the green box to enlarge the window
Open your hand (until you see the "High five!" caption) and then close it
to close the selected window.
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 
Two fingers, Volume:
slide down your finger in the red box to reduce the volume
slide up your finger in the green box to enlarge the volume
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
Three fingers, Scroll:
slide down your finger in the red box to scroll down
slide up your finger in the green box to scroll up
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 
Four fingers, Help:
Just a quick review of this manual
